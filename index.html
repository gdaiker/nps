How Non-Profit Solutions Came to Be:

Non-Profit Solutions is founded and managed by Eric Tillich, a leader in non-profit management. Eric founded Non-Profit Solutions with one simple goal in mind—help non-profits meet their maximum fundraising potential. With over 20 years experience helping non-profits reach new heights in fundraising, he has led fundraising operations in higher education, fine arts and leading human service non-profits. With expertise in annual fund growth, major gift development, grant writing, capital campaigns, strategic planning and donor acquisition Non-Profit Solutions has the tools to help any organization reach its desired goals. Non-Profit Solutions will bring the same passion and drive that you bring to your organization’s mission.
